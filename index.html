About

Hi, I'm Patrícia, an aspiring full-time frontend developer.


I want to code from beautiful design projects and share them through the internet. I have been working as a proofreader for an academic publisher and, as you can imagine, I have to be really focused and detailed to spot all the little things that need to be corrected on the text. It's a work that I love, but I also love coding!


So now, I have the chance to be part of the Front End Web Developer Nanodegree program scholarship sponsored by Bertelsmann and Udacity. It's a great opportunity to learn, develop projects, and be part of a large group of people learning the same as me.


A good motto from Mae Jemison, the first African American female astronaut in space:

Don't let anyone rob you of your imagination, your creativity, or your curiosity. It's your place in the world; it's your life. Go on and do all you can with it, and make it the life you want to live.


So now it's up to me to conquer a place in the front end world!
